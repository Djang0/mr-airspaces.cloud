LFA Golf warnings

Know when you can go higher

Live notifications

Flying in Belgium is not easy. Airspaces architecture is epic, with 28 aerodromes. But sometimes LFA Golf zones are open and pilotes can thermal much higher. Invite me to your Telegram group and I will warn you when these goldy zones are opened.

Use /golf at any time to get status.

openAIR files

Belgium Paragliding Cup (BPC) emits airspaces in openAIR format. I check BPC's Google Drive for openAIR files every 10 minutes and keep trtack of the last 10 files.

You can get it at any time by issuing /oa command.


syntax : /oa

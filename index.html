Living in the clouds · Hosted somewhere in Belgium · On a raspberry PI · Pay my father, a beer!

I am a Telegram bot that ships informations to paragliders aiming to fly in Belgium. I love clouds and wind, maybe we should get in touch on Telegram.

LFA Golf warnings

Know when you can go higher

Live notifications

Flying in Belgium is not easy. Considering our 28 aerodromes, we can easily claim that Belgian airspaces architecture is epic ! But sometimes LFA Golf zones are open and pilotes can go thermaling much higher. Invite me to your Telegram group and I will warn you when these goldy zones are opened, even if you're in a flight.

Use /golf at any time to get the current status.

Source of informations

Liga van Vlaamse Zweefvliegclubs

This organisation reads NOTAM and publish LFA GOLF status.

Chat commands

You can Chat with me directly or through a group chat, use commands and I'll ship you A.I previsions for sites flyability. I monitor 10 paragliding takeoffs on a 10 polling frequency.

I gather data from paraglidable.com and openweathermap.org. If you request it, I'll deliver you an artificial intelligence analysis for the paragliding sites that are currently flyable (wind orientation measures at request time).

openAIR files

Belgium Paragliding Cup (BPC) emits airspaces in openAIR format. I check BPC's Google Drive for openAIR files every 10 minutes and keep track of the last 10 files.

You can get it at any time by issuing /oa command.


syntax : /oa
